Welcome to Tennis Things!

A website dedicated to all things tennis! Bringing you the latest news and updates about upcoming tennis tournaments, iconic rivalries, and the greatest of all time (GOAT) players in the world of tennis.

Stay up to date with the latest schedules, scores, and rankings of tennis tournaments around the world. From the Grand Slams to the smaller, but no less exciting, ATP and WTA events, we will keep you informed about all the action happening on the court.

Relive the greatest moments in tennis history by exploring the iconic rivalries that have captured the hearts and minds of tennis fans around the world. From the epic battles between Rafael Nadal and Roger Federer to the fierce competition between Martina Navratilova and Chris Evert, we will take you on a journey through the most memorable rivalries in tennis history.

And of course, no discussion of tennis would be complete without talking about the greatest players of all time. We will delve into the careers of legends like Roger Federer, Rafael Nadal, Serena Williams, and Steffi Graf to examine what makes them true GOATs in the world of tennis.

Join us as we celebrate the sport of tennis and all the excitement it has to offer. Whether you're a seasoned fan or a newcomer to the game, this website has something for everyone.

Tennis is a big part of my life so I wanted to create a fun websigte for others to connect with the sport!
